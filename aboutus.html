Nu-Tech Engineering & Testing, a company originated in Calgary Alberta had served to numerous clients for both residential and commercial projects in the Geotechnical and Structural Engineering since 1983. The company is specialized in Soil Investigation, Foundation Design, Piles Design, Retaining Wall Design, and Slope Stability Analysis, Structural Design and Structural modification for the factories, and residential home. We vow to use our special knowledge and new technology to come out with the best design and durable structures, still save the cost and meet the expectation for our clients.
